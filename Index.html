2025 Wikipedialympics

The first Wikipedialympics were hosted by Hex Bodelier. They invited their nerdiest friends to compete in four unique challenges tied to popular online encyclopedia Wikipedia. The players, known as the Wikilympians, competed to win the esteemed title of Wikipedialympics Champion.

Background and Conception[edit]

Before organising the event, Bodelier suffered from a long-running fascination for and interest in Wikipedia. They often spent their free time diving into Wikipedia Wormholes, going from article to article to learn interesting facts. They are also a huge fan of the Depths of Wikipedia social media platform, which posts Wikipedia-related content and memes.

Bodelier started keeping a file of noteworthy or interesting articles they came across, and decided to combine their love for Wikipedia with their love for game shows. Thus, the Wikipedialympics were born.

Invitation[edit]

To enlist their friends to compete in the Wikipedialympics, Bodelier sent out digital invitations fashioned to look like a genuine Wikipedia article. Prospective Wikilympians could then respond by filling out a digital form, confirming their participation and setting out on their journey to encyclopaedic legend.

Time and Location[edit]

The Wikipedialympics took place on Saturday, August 2nd, between 14:00 and 16:00 at their apartment. In case it was preferred by the players, the event could also be organised on Friday, August 1st, between 19:00 and 21:00; or on Sunday, August 3rd, between 14:00 and 16:00. Players could indicate this preference in the digital form they received as part of their invitation.

Wikilympians[edit]

Athletes of a different caliber, Wikilympians require fast browsing skills, passion for trivia, and to be fun to be around. Bodelier is quoted as saying “this is going to be a lot of fun, I hope you can all make it!”
